Tech Support Onboarding

Questions? Ask the team in the widget on bottom right of your screen!

  • Directly Platform - SuperAdmin access.
    • As a SuperAdmin, you will be able to create, configure, learn, test, and manage the different aspects of Directly's customer company accounts.
    • To confirm your access, click on your profile icon dropdown and ensure the SuperAdmin option is there

  • Slack - Regular access.
    • Instant messaging tool used for communicating
    • Channels to Join:
      • #tech-support, #development, #devops, #services, #customersuccess

  • Jira - Ticket Queue

  • Confluence - Documentation
    • Spaces: Support, Services, Engineering, Customer Success

  • Helpdesk Portal - Submit Support tickets

  • OpenVPN - required to access certain applications
